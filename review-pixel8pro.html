Google Pixel 8 Pro Review: The Camera That Thinks

📸
Ananya Sharma
Camera & Photography
📅 January 5, 2025 ⏱️ 8 min read
★★★★½8.8/10
📸

Google's Pixel 8 Pro is the rare phone where the whole is greater than the sum of its parts. On paper, the Tensor G3 chip looks average compared to Snapdragon 8 Gen 3. The hardware specs are decent but not class-leading. Yet the overall experience — especially the camera — punches well above what the specifications suggest.

Quick Take: The Pixel 8 Pro is the best camera phone for people who want effortless great photos. The AI processing, 7 years of updates, and pure Android experience make it a compelling buy at ₹84,999 — if you can accept average raw performance.

Design: Premium and Purposeful

The matte back and polished aluminum frame make the Pixel 8 Pro feel expensive without being flashy. The temperature sensor on the back is a quirky addition Google clearly hasn't fully committed to — it works, but third-party app integration is limited. The IP68 rating is standard at this price. The phone is comfortable to hold despite the 6.7-inch size, thanks to well-controlled weight distribution.

Camera: Where This Phone Belongs in a Different League

The triple rear camera system combines a 50MP main sensor, a 48MP ultrawide (with autofocus, enabling macro shots), and a 48MP 5x periscope telephoto. But the real story is what Google's computational photography does with these sensors. Night Sight captures detail in near-darkness that seems physically impossible. Magic Eraser removes unwanted objects from photos with eerie accuracy. Best Take and Photo Unblur are AI features that actually solve real problems. Ananya spent three weeks comparing camera samples with every major competitor — the Pixel wins in challenging conditions more often than not.

What We Liked

  • Best computational photography on any phone
  • 7 years OS + security updates — best in class
  • Pure Android with fast Google updates
  • Temperature sensor (limited but unique)
  • Superb Night Sight in low light
  • IP68 water resistance
  • 30W wireless charging included

What Could Be Better

  • Tensor G3 benchmarks behind SD 8 Gen 3
  • Gets warm under heavy gaming load
  • Some AI features feel early/gimmicky
  • Charging speed (30W) lags competitors
  • Price-to-spec ratio lower than Android rivals
🏆FINAL VERDICT

The Pixel 8 Pro is the phone for photographers, Google software enthusiasts, and anyone who values long-term software support above all else. It's not the fastest Android phone, and it's not the best value. But the camera experience — especially with Google's AI processing — is simply better than anything else for everyday photography. If your camera roll is your priority, this is your phone.

✅ Buy If: Camera quality is your top priority ✅ Buy If: You want 7 years of guaranteed updates ❌ Skip If: Gaming or heavy performance tasks matter ❌ Skip If: You want the fastest-charging phone
