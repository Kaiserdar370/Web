5 Best Wireless Earbuds for Android in 2025 (Tested & Ranked)

👨‍🎮
Rahul Singh
Gaming & Performance
📅 December 16, 2024⏱️ 9 min read
★★★★★Roundup
🎧

I spent three months with 12 pairs of wireless earbuds — using them for commuting, workouts, video calls, gaming, and casual listening. These are my top 5 recommendations for Android users in 2025, ranked from most affordable to premium.

Our Top 5 Ranked

5. CMF Buds Pro 2 (₹3,999) — Best budget ANC earbuds available. Surprisingly good noise cancellation, 42-hour total battery, IPX5. Minor issues with voice call quality but exceptional value. 4. Nothing Ear (a) (₹8,999) — Clean design, balanced sound tuning, good ANC. Best choice if you use a Nothing or CMF phone for ecosystem integration. 3. Samsung Galaxy Buds3 Pro (₹17,999) — Best for Samsung phone users. Deep Galaxy AI integration, excellent ANC, outstanding call quality. Sound profile is V-shaped and energetic. 2. Jabra Elite 8 Active (₹19,999) — Best for workouts and calls. IP57 rating, bone conduction-level call quality, and ANC that blocks gym noise effectively. Not the best for music. 1. Sony WF-1000XM5 (₹24,999 on sale) — Best overall. Industry-leading ANC, exceptional sound quality across all genres, 24-hour battery with case. The benchmark that everything else is measured against.

What We Liked

  • Sony WF-1000XM5 has unmatched ANC and sound quality
  • CMF Buds Pro 2 is extraordinary value at ₹3,999
  • Samsung Buds3 Pro has best Galaxy phone integration
  • Jabra Elite 8 Active is the best workout earbud
  • All 5 recommendations work excellently with Android

What Could Be Better

  • Sony WF-1000XM5 case is large and bulky
  • CMF Buds Pro 2 call quality is average
  • Samsung Buds3 Pro best features require Samsung phones
  • Jabra sound quality behind Sony for music
  • Nothing Ear (a) lacks some premium features
🏆FINAL VERDICT

Our top recommendation: Sony WF-1000XM5 if budget allows; CMF Buds Pro 2 if you want maximum value. Both are exceptional choices for Android users in 2025.

✅ Sony: Best overall earbuds for Android✅ CMF Buds Pro 2: Best budget pick under ₹4,000 ❌ Samsung Buds3 Pro: If you don't use a Samsung phone❌ Jabra: If music quality is your priority
