Motorola Edge 50 Pro Battery Test: 125W Charging in Under 30 Minutes

👨‍💻
Arjun Mehta
Senior Tech Reviewer
📅 December 26, 2024 ⏱️ 8 min read
★★★★8.5/10
🔋

Fast charging has become the defining battleground in mid-range smartphones. Motorola's Edge 50 Pro enters with a claim that sounds impossible: 125W TurboPower charging on a 4500mAh battery. I tested it obsessively.

Quick Take: The 125W charging claim is real and it really does change how you use a smartphone. 0-100% in 28 minutes is extraordinary for a mid-range phone. Add a 144Hz pOLED display, stock-adjacent Android experience, and you have one of the most compelling mid-rangers of 2024-25.

The Charging Test: 28 Minutes, Really

I ran the Edge 50 Pro through 12 charging cycles to average the results. Starting from completely empty (0%), the phone hit 50% in 11 minutes and reached 100% in 28 minutes. That's not a best-case scenario — that's my average result across multiple tests. For context: the iPhone 15 Pro Max takes 90 minutes to charge fully. The Samsung S24 Ultra takes 75 minutes. The OnePlus 13 (which we consider excellent) takes 36 minutes for its larger 6000mAh battery. The Motorola Edge 50 Pro's 28 minutes is genuinely extraordinary for the price.

pOLED Display and Software

The 6.7-inch pOLED display running at 144Hz is one of the best screens in any mid-range phone. OLED punchy colors, 2000 nit peak brightness for outdoor visibility, and 144Hz smoothness make scrolling and media consumption genuinely pleasurable. Motorola's near-stock Android experience (Hello UI) is one of the most refreshing software approaches in the segment — minimal bloatware, fast updates, and clean design. It's the second-best Android software experience after Google's Pixel, in my opinion.

What We Liked

  • 125W charging fills 4500mAh in 28 minutes — extraordinary
  • 144Hz pOLED display is class-leading
  • Near-stock Android with minimal bloatware
  • Good camera system with telephoto
  • IP68 water resistance
  • Wireless charging (50W) is a mid-range rarity

What Could Be Better

  • 4500mAh battery is smaller than competitors at this price
  • Snapdragon 7 Gen 3 performance is solid, not exceptional
  • Camera software needs improvement in low light
  • Motorola's software update schedule has been inconsistent historically
🏆FINAL VERDICT

The Motorola Edge 50 Pro is the right phone if fast charging is the most important thing to you and your budget is around ₹32,000. The combination of 125W wired + 50W wireless charging, a beautiful pOLED display, and clean Android software makes it a standout in a crowded segment.

✅ Buy If: Charging speed is your top priority ✅ Buy If: You want clean Android software ❌ Skip If: Battery capacity matters more than speed ❌ Skip If: Raw gaming performance is key
