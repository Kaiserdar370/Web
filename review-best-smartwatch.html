Best Smartwatches for Android 2025: Galaxy Watch vs Pixel Watch vs OnePlus Watch 2

👨‍💻
Arjun Mehta
Senior Tech Reviewer
📅 December 6, 2024⏱️ 10 min read
★★★★★Roundup
⌚

I wore three smartwatches simultaneously on both wrists for 30 days — the Samsung Galaxy Watch 7, Google Pixel Watch 3, and OnePlus Watch 2. I tracked sleep, workouts, daily steps, heart rate accuracy, and the companion app experience. Here's what I found.

The Definitive 2025 Android Smartwatch Ranking

3rd Place: Google Pixel Watch 3 (₹34,999) — The most beautiful design, cleanest Wear OS experience, and best integration with Pixel phones. Battery life is the killer: 24 hours maximum means you're charging every night without exception. The health sensor accuracy is excellent, but daily battery anxiety holds it back. 2nd Place: OnePlus Watch 2 (₹26,999) — Remarkable battery life (100 hours in smart mode, 30 in full smart mode), excellent build quality, good health tracking. The Wear OS + RTOS hybrid approach means longer battery without sacrificing smart features. Best value smartwatch for Android in 2025. 1st Place: Samsung Galaxy Watch 7 (₹32,999) — The best all-round Android smartwatch. Advanced health features (including body composition analysis), excellent Samsung ecosystem integration, 40+ hour battery life, and Galaxy AI fitness coaching. Galaxy AI's sleep insights and workout analysis are genuinely useful, not just data dumps.

What We Liked

  • Samsung Galaxy Watch 7 wins on features and ecosystem
  • OnePlus Watch 2 has extraordinary 100-hour battery
  • Pixel Watch 3 has the best Wear OS software experience
  • All three have accurate health sensors
  • Galaxy Watch 7 AI fitness coaching is genuinely helpful

What Could Be Better

  • Pixel Watch 3's 24-hour battery is a dealbreaker for many
  • Galaxy Watch 7 best features require Samsung phones
  • OnePlus Watch 2 app ecosystem is limited
  • All three struggle to match Apple Watch for iPhone users
🏆FINAL VERDICT

Samsung Galaxy Watch 7 wins overall for Android users with the best balance of features, battery, and AI coaching. OnePlus Watch 2 is the best value if battery longevity is your priority. Pixel Watch 3 is for Pixel phone users who want deep Google integration.

✅ Galaxy Watch 7: Best all-round Android smartwatch✅ OnePlus Watch 2: Best battery life ❌ Pixel Watch 3: If battery anxiety bothers you❌ Galaxy Watch 7: If you don't use Samsung ecosystem
