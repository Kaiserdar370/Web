POCO X7 Pro Review: Dimensity 9300+ at ₹27,000 — Insane Value

👩‍🔬
Priya Nair
Budget & Mid-Range Expert
📅 January 4, 2025 ⏱️ 7 min read
★★★★8.4/10
🏎️

POCO's strategy has always been simple: take a flagship chip, strip away premium extras, and sell it at a price that makes the competition nervous. The X7 Pro takes this formula further than ever.

Quick Take: The Dimensity 9300+ is a flagship-tier chip. Getting it in a phone at ₹26,999 is extraordinary. Yes, the camera and build cut corners — but if raw performance for gaming and multitasking is your goal, nothing else at this price gets close.

Performance: Flagship Chip, Budget Price

The Dimensity 9300+ inside the POCO X7 Pro is the same chip found in phones costing ₹60,000+. In Geekbench multi-core scores, it trades blows with the Snapdragon 8 Gen 2. In real-world use: BGMI at 90fps locked, Genshin Impact at high settings with playable framerates, and day-to-day app performance that feels instantaneous. For ₹27K, this level of performance shouldn't exist. POCO found a way to make it happen.

The Inevitable Trade-offs

The camera system is the obvious area where costs were cut. The 50MP main sensor is decent in daylight — sharp details, acceptable colors — but falls apart in low light compared to dedicated camera-focused mid-rangers. There's no telephoto lens. The ultrawide is forgettable. If you care about photography, look at the Redmi Note 14 Pro+ instead. The build is plastic-backed rather than glass, and there's no IP rating. POCO UI (based on HyperOS) has bloatware that will frustrate purists.

What We Liked

  • Flagship-grade Dimensity 9300+ chip at mid-range price
  • 6000mAh battery for exceptional longevity
  • 90W fast charging
  • 120Hz AMOLED display looks great
  • Competitive pricing is hard to match

What Could Be Better

  • Camera system is below average at this price
  • No IP rating
  • Plastic back feels less premium
  • POCO UI has significant bloatware
  • Limited software update promise
🏆FINAL VERDICT

If you can live with average cameras and a plastic build, the POCO X7 Pro offers a gaming and performance experience that embarrasses phones at twice the price. It's a spec monster in budget clothing — and that formula has clear appeal for the right buyer.

✅ Buy If: Gaming performance is your #1 priority ✅ Buy If: You want flagship speed under ₹30,000 ❌ Skip If: Camera quality matters to you ❌ Skip If: Build quality and IP rating are important
