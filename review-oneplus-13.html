OnePlus 13 Review: The Fastest Charging Flagship of 2025

👨‍💻
Arjun Mehta
Senior Tech Reviewer
📅 January 11, 2025 ⏱️ 9 min read
★★★★½9.0/10
⚡

OnePlus has always known how to build phones that make you feel something when you use them. The OnePlus 13 continues that tradition with specs that embarrass much more expensive rivals and a charging system so fast it changes your relationship with the charger entirely.

Quick Take: The OnePlus 13 is arguably the best value flagship in India right now. 100W charging that fills the 6000mAh battery in 36 minutes, Snapdragon 8 Elite performance, and a Hasselblad camera system — all starting at ₹69,999. This phone shouldn't exist at this price.

Design: Premium Curves and an Satisfying Heft

The OnePlus 13 has a confidence in its design that some flagships lack. The circular camera module, the textured back (in the Midnight Ocean color we tested), and the satisfying Alert Slider make it feel like a phone with personality. The 6.82-inch display is large but the curved sides and tapered edges make it manageable. At 210g it's substantial but not cumbersome. IP65 water resistance is welcome — not quite IP68, but respectable.

Charging: The 100W System That Changes Everything

0 to 100% in 36 minutes. That's the headline. In practice, the SUPERVOOC 100W charging means you stop thinking about battery anxiety completely. A 15-minute charge in the morning gives you 50% — more than enough to get through a half-day. The 50W wireless charging is the fastest wireless charging on any phone currently available. The reverse wireless charging lets you top up earbuds or a smartwatch. The 6000mAh battery itself lasts nearly two full days with moderate use — combine that with the fast charging, and you have the most stress-free battery experience in the flagship segment.

What We Liked

  • 100W wired charging fills 6000mAh in 36 minutes
  • 50W wireless charging is class-leading
  • Snapdragon 8 Elite performance is unmatched
  • Hasselblad camera tuning produces natural, pleasing images
  • 6000mAh battery for exceptional longevity
  • Alert Slider remains a beloved hardware feature
  • Exceptional value vs competitors

What Could Be Better

  • OxygenOS has moved closer to ColorOS — purists will notice
  • No IP68 (IP65 instead)
  • Ultrawide camera lags behind telephoto quality
  • Microphone quality in calls is average
  • No satellite connectivity features
🏆FINAL VERDICT

The OnePlus 13 is the Android flagship to beat in 2025 for anyone who doesn't need the S24 Ultra's S Pen or Apple's ecosystem. It's faster, charges faster, has a bigger battery, and costs significantly less than both. The Hasselblad camera system is genuinely excellent. OxygenOS is mature and smooth. This is our top Android flagship recommendation under ₹80,000.

✅ Buy If: Charging speed and battery life are priorities ✅ Buy If: You want flagship performance under ₹75,000 ❌ Skip If: You prefer pure OxygenOS (it's changed) ❌ Skip If: IP68 is a requirement
