iPhone 15 Pro Max vs Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra: The Definitive 2024 Flagship Battle

👨‍💻
Arjun Mehta
Senior Tech Reviewer
📅 December 22, 2024⏱️ 12 min read
★★★★★Comparison
🥊

Two of the most expensive, capable smartphones ever made. One from the world's most profitable tech company; the other from the world's largest smartphone maker. After testing both as my daily driver for a month each, here is my honest verdict on which is the better phone — and crucially, for whom.

The Definitive Score

Display: Samsung wins by a slim margin — the anti-reflective Gorilla Glass Armor and 2600 nit brightness edges the iPhone in direct outdoor testing. Performance: Too close to call — the A17 Pro wins synthetic benchmarks; the SD 8 Gen 3 provides superior sustained gaming performance. Camera: The iPhone 15 Pro wins video, decisively. The Samsung S24 Ultra wins telephoto zoom (10x vs 5x) and selfies. Main camera is a tie. Battery: Samsung wins clearly — the 5000mAh cell with better power management outlasts the iPhone by 1.5–2 hours in identical usage tests. Software: Depends on ecosystem loyalty — iOS 17 is more intuitive; One UI 6.1 is more customizable. Value: Samsung at ₹1.30L vs Apple at ₹1.60L — Samsung is better value, objectively. Verdict: Samsung S24 Ultra is the better technical achievement. iPhone 15 Pro Max is the better experience for those inside Apple's ecosystem.

What We Liked

  • Samsung wins display anti-reflection and brightness
  • iPhone 15 Pro Max wins video quality decisively
  • S24 Ultra wins battery life by meaningful margin
  • S24 Ultra wins telephoto zoom capability
  • iPhone wins A17 Pro chip efficiency
  • Samsung's 7-year update promise matches Apple longevity

What Could Be Better

  • Both cost over ₹1.25 lakh — enormous investment
  • Neither has exceptional wired charging speed
  • Samsung is larger and heavier
  • iPhone has no S Pen equivalent
  • Both require ecosystem investment to maximize value
🏆FINAL VERDICT

If you're in the Apple ecosystem and value video, privacy, and seamless device integration — iPhone 15 Pro Max. If you're on Android and want the most capable, versatile single device — Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra. Neither is wrong. Both are extraordinary.

✅ iPhone: If you're in Apple ecosystem✅ Samsung: If you want best Android flagship ❌ Either: If budget is under ₹1.25 lakh❌ iPhone: If S Pen or fast charging matter
