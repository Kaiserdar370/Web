OPPO Find X8 Pro Review: Hasselblad Cameras and a Stunning Display

📸
Ananya Sharma
Camera & Photography
📅 December 24, 2024 ⏱️ 9 min read
★★★★★9.1/10
🌟

OPPO's Find X series has always pushed boundaries in design and photography. The Find X8 Pro arrives as their most refined flagship, carrying Hasselblad's color science from their premium lineup into what is genuinely one of the best camera phones available.

Quick Take: The OPPO Find X8 Pro is the best Android camera phone you've never heard of. Hasselblad's color science produces images with a naturalness and depth that feels different from every other phone camera. Paired with the Dimensity 9400's performance and an exceptional display, this is a serious iPhone and Galaxy competitor.

Hasselblad Cameras: Color Science That Stands Apart

Hasselblad's involvement goes beyond a badge on the camera module. The Natural Colour Calibration system produces images with a warmth and authenticity that instantly distinguishes OPPO Find X8 Pro photos from those of competitors. Where Samsung tends to punch saturation and Apple to optimize for pleasing skin tones, the Find X8 Pro renders scenes with an accuracy closer to what your eye actually saw. For photographers transitioning from dedicated cameras, this color philosophy will feel immediately familiar. The Quad-Tele system — three separate telephoto lenses covering 3x, 6x, and an enhanced periscope — offers versatility that rivals and often exceeds the competition.

Display and Build

The 6.78-inch OLED panel is among the best in any phone — deep blacks, excellent color accuracy in both vivid and natural modes, and 2000 nit peak brightness for outdoor use. The build quality is exceptional: Ceramic Black variant has the satisfying cold-touch feeling of premium ceramics, and the phone feels dense and well-built in a way that communicates quality without screaming for attention. ColorOS running on Android 14 is OPPO's most polished software yet, though it still carries more customization and app overlap than Google or Samsung's approach.

What We Liked

  • Hasselblad color science produces the most natural-looking photos
  • Dimensity 9400 performance matches Snapdragon 8 Elite
  • Exceptional OLED display
  • 5910mAh battery with 80W charging
  • Ceramic build option feels uniquely premium
  • Quad-Tele camera system offers remarkable versatility

What Could Be Better

  • ColorOS has a learning curve for new users
  • Limited physical availability in India
  • No IP68 (IP65 rated)
  • Some AI camera features feel over-processed
  • Less brand recognition than Samsung/Apple at this price
🏆FINAL VERDICT

The OPPO Find X8 Pro is criminally underrated in India. It offers camera quality, performance, and build quality that rivals and sometimes exceeds phones costing ₹30,000–40,000 more. If you're willing to step outside the Samsung-Apple comfort zone, this is an extraordinary flagship.

✅ Buy If: Hasselblad color science appeals to you ✅ Buy If: You want maximum camera versatility ❌ Skip If: Brand recognition matters ❌ Skip If: You prefer stock Android
