ASUS ROG Phone 8 Pro Review: The Ultimate Mobile Gaming Machine

👨‍🎮
Rahul Singh
Gaming & Performance
📅 December 30, 2024 ⏱️ 10 min read
★★★★★9.4/10
🕹️

The ROG Phone 8 Pro is not a phone that tries to be everything to everyone. It's a gaming device that happens to make phone calls. And if gaming is what you care about, it does that better than anything else money can buy.

Quick Take: If you play mobile games seriously, the ROG Phone 8 Pro is in a completely different league from everything else. 165Hz display, ultra-low touch latency, side USB-C port, AirTriggers — ASUS built a controller in phone form. Nothing else comes close for pure gaming capability.

165Hz Display: The Smoothest Screen on Any Phone

The 6.78-inch AMOLED display running at 165Hz is the smoothest smartphone display I have ever used. At this refresh rate, animations feel liquid, and in supported games (BGMI, COD Mobile, Asphalt 9), the difference versus a 120Hz screen is immediately, viscerally noticeable. Touch sampling rate reaches 720Hz in X Mode — the highest on any phone. In competitive FPS gaming, this translates to perceptibly faster input registration. Combined with the AirTriggers (capacitive shoulder buttons that map to touch controls), this setup is as close to a handheld gaming console as a phone gets.

Cooling and Sustained Performance

Thermal management is where gaming phones live or die. The ROG Phone 8 Pro has a vapor chamber cooling system that, combined with the optional AeroActive Cooler 8 accessory (sold separately), maintains peak performance for 60+ minute gaming sessions with minimal throttling. In my tests, running Genshin Impact at Ultra settings for 90 minutes, the phone maintained an average of 58fps — compared to typical flagship phones that drop to 40-45fps after 30 minutes under thermal throttling. The side-mounted USB-C port (in addition to the standard bottom port) means you can charge while gaming without a cable awkwardly dangling from the bottom.

What We Liked

  • 165Hz AMOLED is the smoothest display on any phone
  • AirTriggers provide genuine gaming control advantage
  • Side USB-C port for charging while gaming
  • X Mode squeezes every ounce of performance
  • Excellent thermal management for sustained gaming
  • Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 is the fastest Android chip available
  • Stereo speakers with impressive audio for gaming

What Could Be Better

  • Very large and heavy — not for everyday casual use
  • Camera system is mediocre for a ₹90K phone
  • AeroActive Cooler 8 is sold separately
  • ROG UI takes some adjusting for non-gamers
  • 65W charging is slower than gaming competitors
🏆FINAL VERDICT

If you take mobile gaming seriously, the ROG Phone 8 Pro justifies its price completely. The 165Hz display, AirTriggers, superior cooling, and side-port charging form a gaming package that no other phone manufacturer comes close to. Accept that the camera is not the focus here, and you'll have the best gaming experience available on any mobile device.

✅ Buy If: You're a serious or competitive mobile gamer ✅ Buy If: 165Hz display is important to you ❌ Skip If: Camera quality matters ❌ Skip If: You want a slim, everyday phone
