Tecno Spark 30 Pro Review: Best First Smartphone Under ₹15,000?

👩‍🔬
Priya Nair
Budget & Mid-Range Expert
📅 December 20, 2024 ⏱️ 6 min read
★★★½7.4/10
✨

Tecno has dramatically improved build quality and feature sets across its lineup. The Spark 30 Pro targets first-time smartphone buyers who need reliable daily performance without spending much.

Quick Take: For under ₹14,000, the Tecno Spark 30 Pro offers a 50MP camera, 90Hz display, and a 5000mAh battery that keeps up with daily needs. It's not sophisticated, but it's dependably useful — and for many buyers, that's exactly what they need.

What You Get for Under ₹14,000

The Spark 30 Pro makes sensible choices: a 6.78-inch IPS display at 90Hz looks decent for media consumption. The 5000mAh battery with 18W charging means you'll charge overnight but won't worry about running out mid-day. The Helio G88 chip handles social media, WhatsApp, YouTube, and basic photography without complaint. Don't expect to game at high settings — that's not what this phone is for.

Camera Honesty

The 50MP camera claim requires context: it's a 50MP sensor with an f/1.6 aperture, which sounds impressive. In daylight, photos are sharp with acceptable colors. The AI processing occasionally oversaturates or over-sharpens, but the results are usable and better than entry-level phones from 2-3 years ago. Night photography is where budget hardware shows its limits — details smear and noise increases significantly. For photos of food, family gatherings, and outdoor daylight scenes, this works. For serious photography, it doesn't.

What We Liked

  • Very affordable at ₹13,499
  • 90Hz display is smooth for the price
  • 5000mAh battery handles a full day
  • Decent daylight camera
  • Good build quality for the price

What Could Be Better

  • Helio G88 is limited for gaming
  • 18W charging is slow
  • Night photography is weak
  • No stereo speakers
  • Limited software update support expected
🏆FINAL VERDICT

The Tecno Spark 30 Pro is a competent, affordable first smartphone. It won't wow you with any single feature, but it does the basics reliably at an accessible price. For first-time buyers, students, or as a secondary device, it earns a reasonable recommendation.

✅ Buy If: This is your first smartphone ✅ Buy If: Budget is under ₹15,000 ❌ Skip If: You need gaming performance ❌ Skip If: Camera quality is important
