Samsung S24 Ultra vs OnePlus 13 Battery Life: 72-Hour Real World Test Results

👨‍💻
Arjun Mehta
Senior Tech Reviewer
📅 December 18, 2024⏱️ 8 min read
★★★★Test
⚡

Battery comparisons between phones of different generations and prices are often misleading because they rely on synthetic loopback video tests. Real life is messier — and more informative. I ran both the OnePlus 13 and Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra through identical 72-hour usage patterns and tracked every drain.

The Results: OnePlus 13 Wins by More Than Expected

Day 1 (Heavy): 6 hours screen-on-time, 45 minutes BGMI, 2 hours streaming, navigation for 30 minutes. OnePlus 13 ended Day 1 at 31%. Samsung S24 Ultra ended Day 1 at 18%. Day 2 (Moderate): 4 hours screen-on, social media, calls, messaging. OnePlus 13 ended Day 2 with 14% remaining. Samsung S24 Ultra needed a top-up charge mid-day. Day 3 (Light): Email, calls, occasional browsing. OnePlus 13 lasted to 11pm. Samsung needed charging by 7pm. The larger 6000mAh battery in the OnePlus 13 combined with the Snapdragon 8 Elite's superior power efficiency is a decisive combination. The S24 Ultra's 5000mAh cell is adequate, but the OnePlus wins every scenario by a meaningful margin. Charging: OnePlus 13 100W vs S24 Ultra 45W — OnePlus charges from 0-100% in 36 minutes; Samsung takes 75 minutes. Not even close.

What We Liked

  • OnePlus 13 wins every battery scenario tested
  • 100W vs 45W charging is not close
  • OnePlus' 6000mAh cell provides a cushion for power users
  • SD 8 Elite power efficiency is exceptional
  • OnePlus 13 costs ₹60,000 less than S24 Ultra

What Could Be Better

  • S24 Ultra has better display and camera versatility
  • S Pen is unique to Samsung
  • Galaxy AI features are more developed
  • 7-year update promise is S24 Ultra's advantage
  • OnePlus IP65 vs Samsung IP68
🏆FINAL VERDICT

For battery life and charging speed, the OnePlus 13 wins this comparison convincingly — and at ₹60,000 less than the S24 Ultra. If battery longevity is your primary concern, the choice is clear. But the S24 Ultra is a more complete flagship in other respects.

✅ OnePlus 13: Battery and charging winner✅ S24 Ultra: Camera versatility and AI features ❌ S24 Ultra: If battery is your top priority❌ OnePlus 13: If S Pen matters to you
